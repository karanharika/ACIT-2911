LilMusicano

Lil Musicano is here for all your music needs. Finding music has become a lot more easier with this free music app. It is a versatile application with a modern, simple and easy to use Design UI. It gives you a variety of music with its special 'Top 10' feature that always keep you in touch with the new trending songs. Its 'My Music-Beta' feature allows you to upload any song of your choice that gives you the best option if you want to combine your own collection with an online service.

Finding music has become a lot more fun and easier with its free music streaming services.

To stream top 10 music click on the Top 10 at the top in the Navigation bar.
